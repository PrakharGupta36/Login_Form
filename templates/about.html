About Us

We are a forward-thinking company specializing in harnessing green energy affordably, with a focus on innovative technology, business development, and assessment. Our key roles include:

  • Technology: Pioneering advancements in hardware, IoT, analytical tools, data science, machine learning, AI, AWS, and software solutions.
  • Business Development: Focusing on market expansion and strategic partnerships.

We operate within a dynamic environment, actively managing technical and political risks:

  • Technical Risks: Addressing technology challenges, operation, maintenance, and human resources.
  • Political Risks: Navigating policy, licensing, commissioning, and approval challenges.

We follow an agile process that emphasizes continuous R&D, market delivery, and feedback mechanisms:

  • Continuous R&D and Delivery: Responding to market needs and guiding our R&D efforts.
  • Flexibility: Adapting to shifts in core technology, adoption curves, and market feedback.
  • Lean Manufacturing System: Applying DMAIC principles (Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control) to enhance our products and services portfolio.
